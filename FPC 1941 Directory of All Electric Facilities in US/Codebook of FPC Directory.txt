Codebook for the 1941 FPC Directory of Electric Facilities in the US
Compnay Name: Company Name
State: State of Company
Company Code: Zip Code of Company
Electric Utility: The electric utility in the facility in dollars
Electric Operating Revenues: The operating revenues of the facility in dollars
Electric Customers: Number of customers
Energy Purchased: The amount of Energy purchased by the facility in KWH
Energy Generated: The amount of Energy generated by the facility in KWH
